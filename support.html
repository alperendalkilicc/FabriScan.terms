‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the fabric analysis?
FabriScan uses advanced AI technology to provide highly accurate fabric analysis. Our system can identify fabric types, material composition, weaving techniques, and quality assessments with professional-grade precision.
What types of fabrics can FabriScan analyze?
FabriScan can analyze virtually all fabric types including cotton, silk, wool, polyester, linen, denim, and blended materials. The app works with both natural and synthetic fabrics.
Do I need premium subscription to use the app?
FabriScan offers premium subscriptions for unlimited analyses and advanced features. You can choose between weekly or yearly subscription plans to get the most out of the app.
How do I take the best photos for analysis?
For best results, take photos in good lighting, ensure the fabric fills most of the frame, avoid shadows, and capture the fabric texture clearly. The app works best with close-up, well-lit photos.
Can I save my analysis results?
Yes! FabriScan automatically saves your analysis history. You can view all your previous analyses in the History section and access detailed information anytime.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription anytime through your iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > FabriScan. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely! We take your privacy seriously. Your photos and analysis results are processed securely and are not shared with third parties. Please read our Privacy Policy for more details.

The app crashes when I try to take a photo
Make sure you've granted camera permissions to FabriScan in your iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera. If the issue persists, try restarting the app or your device.
Analysis is taking too long
Analysis time depends on your internet connection. Make sure you have a stable internet connection. If the analysis seems stuck, try closing and reopening the app.
I can't access premium features
If you've purchased a subscription but can't access premium features, try restoring your purchases in the app or check your subscription status in iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
